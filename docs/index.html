01 — FEATURE ENGINEERING

22 Raw Signals.
11 Derived Insights.

Six behavioral categories extracted from raw on-chain history, engineered into high-signal creditworthiness features.

⏱
Activity & History
Wallet age, total transaction count, active days — establishing temporal credibility and usage consistency.
✓
Repayment Behavior
On-time vs late vs defaulted loan ratio. Weighted repayment quality score accounting for volume and consistency.
◈
Liquidity Signals
Average ETH balance, balance volatility, stability score. Penalizes erratic balance patterns that predict default risk.
⬡
Protocol Interaction
Protocols used, chains active on, governance votes cast. Cross-protocol breadth signals long-term DeFi commitment.
⚠
Risk Signals
Liquidation events, flash loan usage ratio. Default severity combines default rate × liquidation exposure — top predictor at 31.7% importance.
◎
DeFi Yield Behavior
LP positions, staking duration, NFT holdings. Active yield participation signals long-term protocol alignment.
